Buying a Bike:

  1. Google search "urban bike companies"
  2. choose Big Shark (first company to come up)
  3. Select "Shop" in navigation
  4. Within Shop, go to "Need a New Bike?"
  5. Scroll over "style"
  6. scroll and click on "Hybrid/Commuter Bikes"
  7. Choose the 200-499.99 price range on the left side
  8. Go through the options and browse by color/savings
  9. open Schwinn Traveler 2016 in new tab
  10. open Raleigh Wilder 2012 in new tab
  11. open Schwinn Brighton 2 in new tab
  12. Compare look and speed of the three bikes. Preference to brand with multiple-speed bikes
  13. Go back and filter by size (Small) and gender (women's)
  14. Look through bikes, looking for blue or black bikes
  15. choose Schwinn Brighton 2
  16. Check speed/gear settings (this was a 3-speed)
  17. zoomed in on details of bike
  18. Go back to Google and research Schwinn Brighton's price at other stores
  19. choose "Add to Cart" back on the bike page
  20. click on the shopping cart in the top right corner
  21. Make the order!
